Chris Danielson - Route Setting Bio     (interview)
With over 12 years experience as a routesetter, Chris has worked commercially in numerous climbing gyms in the country, including the Boulder Rock Club, Metrorock, and Sportrock, and dozens of other gyms as a competition routesetter.Beginning with ASCF Regional Difficulty competitions in 1995, Chris developed his setting skills through years of practice assisting at high level competitions like the Teva Games, ABS Regionals and Nationals, and PCA’s. Specializing in bouldering, Chris now sets across the country at the professional level, most recently as Head Setter for the 2006 SLC Sendfest and 2007 ABS Nationals. For the past two years, he has been working as an independent contractor and consultant in the climbing industry, providing start-up and operations consulting to all types of climbing facilities. He has also been involved in the design and installation of climbing walls as a construction supervisor for Eldorado Wall Company, and is the former Operations Director for USA Climbing. Chris is the National Climbing Gym Rep for GTHI, representing e-Grips, Flashed, PMI, and Trango, and can be reached at cdanielson@gthi.com.
