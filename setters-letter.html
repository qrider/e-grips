Letter to the Setter:


Dear Setter,

We create so you can create. At e-Grips, your work is what we are passionate about and the motivation that drives us comes from years of routesetting experience, climbing adventures, and artistic vision. Just as you start with blank walls, concentrating on innovative ways to use terrain and build the framework for interesting climbing movement, our shapers stare down at a lifeless block of foam with the goal of creating something from nothing. Imagination inspires creation.

As artists, whether you are setting a beginner route at your local gym, or a finals problem at a World Cup, we know you are building movement to inspire. When you chalk up fresh holds and see your creations climbed for the first time, you are watching as people learn something new or are challenged to push the limits of their ability. We believe that you, as the setter, can make every route an exciting experience for the climber and every singular motion a lesson in movement.

Our holds are shaped to enhance your creative abilities, and in that process we also think constructively about the preferences of climbers. Each design incorporates a technical consideration for the functional use of the hold, a unique aesthetic, and is held to the highest standards of structural integrity, textural consistency, and overall quality. Our crimps are comfortable, our slopers friendly, and our pockets hospitable.

If it were not for you, the walls would be bare and the gyms would be empty. You design the landscape from a blank slate, and sustain the energy that motivates us to become better climbers, to train for our projects, and to teach our kids. At e-Grips, we make handholds as tools for your artistic inspiration, and support a culture of innovation and creativity in climbing.

Thank You!
