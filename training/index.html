eGRIPS Pure Power Training
5 holds per set, with perfectly uniform grabs. Buy duplicate sets, and create your own training wall design to train specific grip positions or repeated movements.  
Pure Power Training holds are intended for use according to your specific interests and training goals. Versatility is important in any training and these shapes encourage great flexibility in training. They are also great for regular routesetting!

Suggested set-up:
- Any wall angle can be used, but between 20 and 60 degree is advised for power training.
- Use duplicates of each hold in a linear fashion to create similar left/right movements. (Suggested minimum of 4 of each set you intend to train movements with)

eGRIPS System Tile Training
6 Basic movements, 6 primary hand postions, and 6 angles of training. System Tile Training is the orginal concept to combine strength training and exercise science.  
System Training Tiles provide multiple grip positions in each tile that can create the perfect strength training wall for climbers of various ability levels. Choose holds and angle based on your weaknesses or needs.

Suggested set-up:
- 20 degree wall. Each vertical half should mirror exactly the other half. Don't favor one side over the other, or the imbalance will haunt you.
- 3 sets of foot holds.
- 2 matching tiles of each grip desired [suggested minimum of 16 tiles total + foot holds]
